02 / Who

Who is
HYPRLUV

HYPRLUV is the solo project of a producer, vocalist, and full time noise enthusiast making hyperpop for the year 2000 that never quite arrived. Born online, raised in the club, built to be played far too loud.

Since the first bedroom upload in 2023, HYPRLUV has gone from blown out laptop speakers to sold out rooms across three continents, turning sugar rush melodies and system crash drums into a sound that refuses to sit still.
